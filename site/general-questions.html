General Questions

Use these for speaking practice. The goal is shorter, clearer structure, not long wandering answers.

1. Tell me about yourself.

What to look for: 30 to 60 seconds, Zoom + Amazon, backend / distributed systems / APIs / OAuth, and what he wants next.

Good structure:

  1. who I am
  2. what I worked on recently
  3. what I want next

2. Tell me about a production issue you debugged.

What to look for: symptom, investigation, root cause, fix, follow-up / prevention.

Helpful follow-ups:

  • What was the symptom first?
  • How did you narrow it down?
  • What was the fix?
  • What changed afterward?

3. Can you walk me through the S3 to MySQL migration?

What to look for: why S3 was not enough, what problem needed to be fixed, why MySQL helped, rollout / migration safety, and result / impact.

Helpful follow-ups:

  • What was wrong with the original setup?
  • Why was MySQL a better fit here?
  • How did you avoid breaking production?
