# General Questions

## Core Questions To Practice Often

### Tell me about yourself.

What to look for:
- 30 to 60 seconds
- Zoom + Amazon
- backend / distributed systems / APIs / OAuth
- what he wants next

Good structure:
1. who I am
2. what I worked on recently
3. what I want next

### Tell me about a production issue you debugged.

What to look for:
- symptom
- investigation
- root cause
- fix
- follow-up / prevention

Helpful follow-ups:
- What was the symptom first?
- How did you narrow it down?
- What was the fix?
- What changed afterward?

### Can you walk me through the S3 to MySQL migration?

What to look for:
- why S3 was not enough
- what problem needed to be fixed
- why MySQL helped
- rollout / migration safety
- result / impact

Helpful follow-ups:
- What was wrong with the original setup?
- Why was MySQL a better fit here?
- How did you avoid breaking production?

## Additional Useful Questions

- Walk me through your resume.
- Tell me about an on-call or PagerDuty incident you handled.
- Can you explain one project where you had strong ownership?
- What part of the OAuth platform did you own?
- Have you used AI tools in your work or learning?
- What parts of AI tools have been genuinely useful to you?
