At Zoom, the OAuth app provisioning workflow required multiple teams to manually review and configure each new app. The process took several days. We redesigned and automated the workflow: app lookup, configuration, and token provisioning were all automated through internal tooling. The result was provisioning time went from days to under 5 minutes. The hardest part was mapping out every manual step that existed in the old process — a lot of it was tribal knowledge that nobody had documented. Lesson: before automating anything, you have to fully understand the current manual process. Shadow the people doing it. Write it down. Only then can you automate it correctly.
